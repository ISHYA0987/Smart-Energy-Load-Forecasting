Smart Energy Consumption Forecasting

This project leverages a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) deep learning model to forecast future energy consumption based on historical usage patterns. The system helps users analyze predicted energy demand, identify peak load periods, and make informed decisions to improve energy efficiency.

🎯 Problem Statement

Understanding the need for intelligent energy forecasting.

Challenges

Traditional energy management systems rely on historical observations and manual planning, making it difficult to accurately predict future electricity demand. This often leads to inefficient energy utilization, increased operational costs, and difficulty in identifying peak load periods.

Proposed Solution

This project applies a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network to learn temporal energy consumption patterns and generate accurate 24-hour forecasts. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations, identifies peak consumption periods, and supports smarter energy management decisions.

🚀 Key Features

Core functionalities of the Smart Energy Forecasting System.

24-Hour Forecasting

Predicts electricity consumption for the next 24 hours using historical energy usage patterns.

LSTM Deep Learning

Utilizes a two-layer LSTM neural network to learn temporal dependencies in energy data.

Interactive Dashboard

Provides KPI cards, charts and visual insights for monitoring forecasted energy consumption.

Peak Load Detection

Automatically identifies high energy demand periods for better planning and optimization.

Smart Recommendations

Suggests optimal appliance usage based on predicted energy demand and load levels.

Model Analytics

Displays performance metrics, feature importance and training history for analysis.
