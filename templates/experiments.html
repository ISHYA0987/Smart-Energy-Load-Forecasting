🔍 Key Findings

Major observations from the experimentation process.

Multiple Configurations Evaluated

Tested baseline, reduced feature set and multiple sequence lengths to identify the most effective forecasting model.

Longer Sequence ≠ Better Performance

Increasing the sequence length to 48 did not improve forecasting accuracy and introduced additional model complexity.

Best Accuracy Achieved

Sequence length 12 produced the lowest MAE and RMSE while achieving the highest R² score among all experiments.

Final Model Selection

The Seq12 configuration was selected as the production model because it provided the best balance between prediction accuracy and computational efficiency.
